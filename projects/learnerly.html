Create your e-learning platform in seconds using learnerly!

- idea -

Learnerly is an innovative e-learning platform designed to empower educators and content creators. Our product allows anyone to generate their own e-learning platform, with complete customization of fonts, logos, and colors. In just seconds, users can have a fully functional platform where they can stream courses and start earning. Our motto is simple: "We earn when you earn." Learnerly charges only a fraction of what users earn, ensuring zero risk—if they don't earn, we don't charge. This approach provides a low-risk, high-reward opportunity for educators to share their knowledge and monetize their content effectively. My role in this project was to lead the design team as a User Experience lead and create a coming soon page until all designs have accessible UI.
