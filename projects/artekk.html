ARtekk: Immersive Augmented Reality Experiences

- idea -

ARtekk is my favorite project, designed to bring an immersive and straightforward augmented reality (AR) and 3D experience to various fields. We have created 12 verticals, including lens, clothes, footwear, cap, makeup, jewelry, furniture, watches, cars, newspapers, and billboards. Each vertical allows users to explore and interact with products in a cool and engaging way. ARtekk's innovative approach transforms how people experience these items, providing a unique and captivating user experience.
